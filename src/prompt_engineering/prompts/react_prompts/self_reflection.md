Before taking your next action, pause and reflect on your progress so far.

<self-reflection>
Answer each of the following questions honestly and concisely:

1. **Goal check**: What is the user's core objective? Am I still on track to achieve it?
2. **Action review**: Was my last action productive? Did it bring me closer to delivering a complete, high-quality lesson?
3. **Mistake identification**: Did I make any errors or sub-optimal choices? For example:
   - Did I look up vocabulary that is irrelevant to the user's context?
   - Did I repeat a tool call unnecessarily?
   - Did I miss an important topic the user asked about?
4. **Knowledge gaps**: What information am I still missing to complete the task?
5. **Budget awareness**: How many tool calls do I have remaining? Can I finish within budget?
6. **Strategy adjustment**: Based on the above, should I change my approach? If so, how?

Summarize your reflection in 2-3 sentences, then proceed with your next Thought → Action step.
</self-reflection>
